Welcome to Y-Hack 2013.

What is Y-Hack?

Y-Hack is a national hackathon hosted by and at Yale University. The goal of the event is to bring together student hackers and entrepreneurs with experienced industrial innovators.

What can I win?

We offer three tracks: a free development track, the Microsoft Bing track, and the Intel HTML5 track.

For the free development track, there will be five cash awards of $3500, $1500, $750, $500, and $100.

For each of the two API development tracks, the sponsoring company will provide secret specialized prizes. Sign-up for the API tracks is limited, so apply early to nab your spot!

Be on the lookout for more nifty hardware prizes such as Samsung Galaxy Tabs, Google Chromecasts, and more, courtesy of our generous sponsors, to be announced.

Who can come?

You! Anyone currently enrolled in university is available to enter Y-Hack with confirmed registration.

I don't code, what about me?

You've found the perfect place and time to learn! But just remember that we'll only reimburse travel if you actually demo a project.

Welcome to Y-Hack 2013.

Why hack at Y-Hack?

Y-Hack is a national hackathon hosted by and at Yale University. The goal of the event is to bring together student hackers and entrepreneurs with experienced industrial innovators.

    Who can come?

    You! Anyone currently enrolled in a bachelor's program is available to enter Y-Hack with confirmed registration.

    Not in college but still want to participate? Go ahead and register but send us an email explaining your circumstances. Decisions will be made on a case-by-case basis.

    Can I register as a team?

    Absolutely! We encourage people to register in teams of four. Just include your team members' names on the registration page. And don't forget that each team member much submit a separate registration!

    I don't have a team :(

    No worries - there will be time for groups of less than 4 to form teams throughout registration.

    I don't code, what about me?

    You've found the perfect place and time to learn! But just remember that we'll only reimburse travel if you actually demo a project.

    I live too far away, what can I do?

    Don't worry, we'll reimburse up to $100 of your transportation costs, no questions asked. If you need more, please feel free to reach out to us and we can work something out.
